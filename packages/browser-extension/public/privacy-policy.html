Think-Prompt for Web — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-20.

What we collect

When you submit a prompt on a supported LLM website (ChatGPT, Claude.ai, Gemini, Perplexity, Genspark), the extension reads the text of that prompt.

Capture only happens at submit time (button click or Enter). We do not observe keystrokes or draft text.

Where the data goes

Nowhere outside your device. The extension stores captured prompts in your browser's IndexedDB and, optionally, forwards them to the Think-Prompt agent listening on http://127.0.0.1:47823 (localhost on your own machine).

The extension contains no external network calls. You can audit this in the source at github.com/must-goldenrod/think-prompt.

PII masking

Before anything is written — including the IndexedDB copy — common PII is replaced with tokens on-device: email addresses, Korean resident registration numbers, phone numbers, credit cards, API keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, AWS, GitHub), JWTs, IPv4 addresses.

What we never do

  • Send data to any third-party server.
  • Read pages outside the whitelisted LLM sites and localhost.
  • Modify the pages you visit.
  • Use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale.

How to remove the data

Uninstalling the extension removes the IndexedDB store. If you run the agent, think-prompt wipe --yes removes everything from ~/.think-prompt/ as well.
